11.1 Concentration of Solutions

The Concentration of a solution is the amount of solute present in a given quantity of solvent or solution.

विलायक की निश्चित मात्रा में घुले हुए विलेय की मात्रा को, उस विलयन की सान्द्रता (Concentration) कहते हैं।

1. Mass Percentage (w/w%)

Mass of solute per 100g of solution.

Mass % =
Mass of solute Mass of solution
× 100

2. Volume Percentage (v/v%)

Volume of solute per 100ml of solution.

Volume % =
Vol of solute Vol of solution
× 100

3. Mass/Volume % (w/v%)

Mass of solute (g) in 100ml of solution.

Commonly used in Pharmacy and Medicine.

4. Parts Per Million (PPM)

Used when solute is present in trace quantities.

PPM =
Parts of component Total parts of solution
× 10⁶

11.2 Mole Fraction, Molality & Molarity

1. Mole Fraction (𝑥) - मोल अंश

The ratio of the number of moles of a particular component to the total number of moles of all the components.

xA =
nA nA + nB
     xA + xB = 1

🧮 Lab: Mole Fraction Visualizer

Total sum (xA + xB) = 1.00

2. Molality (m) - मोललता

Number of moles of solute dissolved in 1 kilogram (1000g) of solvent.

1 kg विलायक में उपस्थित विलेय के मोलों की संख्या।
Molality (m) =
Mass of solute (w) × 1000 Molar Mass × Mass of solvent (W in g)
💡 TEMPERATURE INDEPENDENT: Since mass does not change with temperature.

3. Molarity (M) - मोलरता

Number of moles of solute dissolved in 1 Litre of solution.

एक लीटर विलयन में घुले हुए विलेय के मोलों की संख्या।
Molarity (M) =
Mass of solute (w) × 1000 Molar Mass × Volume of solution (V in ml)
⚠️ TEMPERATURE DEPENDENT: Volume changes with temperature, so Molarity changes!

🧪 The Dilution Equation

When adding solvent (dilution), the number of moles of solute remains constant.

M₁V₁ = M₂V₂

M₁, V₁ = Molarity & Volume of stock solution.
M₂, V₂ = Molarity & Volume of diluted solution.

11.4 Normality & Advanced Units

🔹 Normality (N) - नॉर्मलता

The number of gram equivalents of solute dissolved in 1 Litre of solution.

Normality (N) =
Mass of solute (w) × 1000 Equivalent Weight (E) × Volume (V in ml)
विलेय के ग्राम तुल्यांको की संख्या, जो एक लीटर विलयन में घुली होती है।

🔹 Formality (F) - फॉर्मलता

The number of gram formula masses of solute dissolved in 1 Litre of solution. Used for ionic compounds.

Formality (F) =
Mass of solute (w) × 1000 Formula Weight (F.w.) × Volume (V in ml)

⚖️ Formulas for Equivalent Weight (E):

For Acid =
Molar Mass Number of replaceable H⁺ (Basicity)
For Base =
Molar Mass Number of replaceable OH⁻ (Acidity)
For Salt =
Molar Mass Total Positive or Negative Charge
For Element =
Atomic Mass Valency
For redox Agent =
Molar Mass Number of e⁻ Gained or Lost per molecule
