Part 5: Dimensional Analysis (विमीय विश्लेषण)

Definition & Principle (परिभाषा एवं सिद्धांत)

When a derived unit of a physical quantity is expressed in terms of exponents of base units, those exponents are called the Dimensions (विमा) of that quantity.

💡 Use of Dimensional Analysis: With the help of dimensional analysis, one unit of a physical quantity is converted into another unit. This is also called the 'Unit Factor Method' (इकाई गुणांक विधि).

Important Conversion Factors (रूपांतरण गुणांक)

(i) 1 Calorie (Cal)= 4.186 J
(v) 1 Å (Angstrom)= 10⁻¹⁰ m
(ii) 1 Dyne= 10⁻⁵ N
(vi) 1 Micron (μm)= 10⁻⁶ m
(iii) 1 Erg= 10⁻⁷ J
(vii) 1 Nanometer (nm)= 10⁻⁹ m
(iv) 1 eV (Electron Volt)= 1.602 × 10⁻¹⁹ J
(viii) 1 bar= 10⁵ Pa

Solved Examples (महत्वपूर्ण उदाहरण)

Example 1: Å to pm conversion

Q: Convert 0.50 Å into picometers (pm).

Step 1: 1 Å = 10⁻¹⁰ m
Step 2: 1 pm = 10⁻¹² m
Step 3: 0.50 Å = 0.50 × 10⁻¹⁰ m
       = 0.50 × 10⁻¹⁰ × 10¹² pm
       = 0.50 × 10² pm
Ans: 50 pm

Example 2: Inch to cm conversion

Q: A piece of metal is 3 inches long. What is its length in cm?

Step 1: 1 inch = 2.54 cm
Step 2: 3 inches = 3 × 2.54 cm
Ans: 7.62 cm

Example 3: Litre to m³ conversion

Q: A jug contains 2L of milk. Calculate its volume in m³.

Step 1: 1 L = 1000 cm³
Step 2: 1000 L = 1 m³
Step 3: 1 L = 1/1000 m³
Step 4: 2 L = 2 × 10⁻³ m³
Ans: 2 × 10⁻³ m³

Example 4: Time Conversion

Q: How many seconds are there in 2 days?

Step 1: 1 day = 24 hours
Step 2: 1 hour = 60 minutes
Step 3: 1 minute = 60 seconds
Step 4: Total = 2 × 24 × 60 × 60
Ans: 1,72,800 s
