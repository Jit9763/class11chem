3.1 Measurement of Physical Quantities (भौतिक राशियों का मापन)

🔹 Physical Quantity (भौतिक राशि)

Quantities which can be measured or weighed are called physical quantities.

Ex: Time, Work, Mass, Length, Volume, etc.

🔹 Unit (मात्रक)

The fixed Standard (मानक) selected to measure a physical quantity is called its Unit.

📏 Systems of Units (मात्रकों की पद्धतियाँ):

1. MKS System
  • Length: Meter
  • Mass: Kilogram
  • Time: Second
2. CGS System
  • Length: Centimeter
  • Mass: Gram
  • Time: Second
3. FPS System
  • Length: Foot
  • Mass: Pound
  • Time: Second

🌍 SI System (The International System of Units)

This is the international system of units, effectively a modernized version of the MKS System.

✔ 7 Base Units (मूल मात्रक)
✔ Derived Units (व्युत्पन्न मात्रक)
✔ Supplementary Units (पूरक मात्रक)

🚀 Modern Redefinition (2019)

  • 🔹 kg: Planck Constant (h)
  • 🔹 A: Elementary Charge (e)
  • 🔹 K: Boltzmann Constant (k)
  • 🔹 mol: Avogadro Constant (NA)

3.4 Standard Definitions

1. Meter:

Light path in vacuum in 1/299,792,458 s.

2. Kilogram:

Mass of 1L water at 4°C.

3. Second:

9,192,631,770 periods of Cs-133 radiation.

4. Ampere:

2 × 10⁻⁷ N/m force between two wires.

🌡️ 3.8 तापमान (Temperature) एवं लाइव सिमुलेटर

Heat intensity measurement and scale inter-conversion.

Celsius (°C) (सेल्सियस)

हिमांक: 0° / क्वथनांक: 100°

Fahrenheit (°F) (फारेनहाइट)

हिमांक: 32° / क्वथनांक: 212°

Kelvin (K) (केल्विन)

S.I. Unit (SI मात्रक - परम ताप)

तैयार: मान दर्ज करें (Ready: Enter values...)

📏 तापमान के पैमाने (Scales):

  • 🌡️ Celsius (°C): Water freezes at 0° and boils at 100°. (0° से 100° के बीच आधारित)।
  • 🌡️ Fahrenheit (°F): Freezing point 32° and boiling point 212°. (हिमांक 32° और क्वथनांक 212°)।
  • 🌡️ Kelvin (K): SI Unit. Absolute Temp (परम ताप). (इसमें ऋण मान नहीं होता)।

🧠 मुख्य अवधारणाएँ (Special Insights):

  • ❄️ Absolute Zero: 0 K or -273.15 °C. (परम शून्य ताप)।
  • ⚖️ Formula: °F = 9/5 (°C) + 32
  • ❌ Warning: Kelvin scale never uses the degree (°) sign. (केल्विन में डिग्री का चिन्ह नहीं लगता)।
