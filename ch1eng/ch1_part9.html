Part 9: Mole Concept & Molar Mass

9.1 The Mole - A Fundamental Unit

Amount of Substance is a base physical quantity in the S.I. system, and its unit is the 'Mole'.

पदार्थ की मात्रा (Amount of Substance) एक मूल भौतिक राशि होती है जिसका S.I. मात्रक 'मोल' (Mole) होता है।
What is 1 Mole?
1 mole = 6.02214076 × 10²³ particles

This number is known as the Avogadro Constant (NA).

9.2 Definitions of 1 Mole (मोल की परिभाषाएं)

1. Based on Carbon-12:

One mole is the amount of substance that contains as many entities as there are atoms in exactly 12 g (0.012 kg) of the Carbon-12 isotope.

किसी पदार्थ की वह मात्रा, जिसमें उतने ही कण होते हैं जितने शुद्ध C-12 समस्थानिक के 12 g में परमाणुओं की संख्या होती है।

2. Based on Mass:

The mass of 1 mole of a substance is equal to its Atomic or Molecular Mass expressed in Grams.

किसी पदार्थ की द्रव्यमान संख्या के बराबर ग्रामों में ली गई मात्रा, 1 मोल कहलाती है।
Example: 1 mole CO₂ = 44 g CO₂ = 6.022 × 10²³ molecules

9.3 Molar Volume & Molar Mass

🎈 Molar Volume (मोलर आयतन)

At NTP/STP, the volume occupied by 1 mole of any gas is exactly 22.4 Litres.

Volume at NTP = 22.4 L

⚖️ Molar Mass (मोलर द्रव्यमान)

The mass of one mole of a substance in grams is called its molar mass.

Percentage Composition (प्रतिशत संघटन)

The percentage by mass of each element in a compound is known as its Percentage Composition.

किसी यौगिक में उपस्थित विभिन्न तत्वों की प्रतिशत मात्रा को उस यौगिक का प्रतिशत संघटन कहते हैं।
Mass % of an Element =
Mass of that element in the compound Molar mass of the compound
× 100

Calculation for NaCl (Salt)

Molar Mass of NaCl = 23 (Na) + 35.5 (Cl) = 58.5 g

% of Na =
23 58.5
× 100 = 39.31%

% of Cl =
35.5 58.5
× 100 = 60.69%

Empirical & Molecular Formula (मूलानुपाती और आण्विक सूत्र)

📌 Empirical Formula:

Represents the simplest whole number ratio of atoms of various elements present in a compound.

📌 Molecular Formula:

Shows the actual number of atoms of each element present in a molecule of the compound.

🧪 Advanced Problem: Formula Calculation

Question: A compound contains 4.07% hydrogen, 24.27% carbon, and 71.65% chlorine. Its molar mass is 98.96 g. Find its Empirical and Molecular Formula.

Step 1: Convert % to grams (assuming 100g)
H = 4.07 g, C = 24.27 g, Cl = 71.65 g

Step 2: Calculate moles
H = 4.07/1.008 = 4.04 mol | C = 24.27/12.01 = 2.021 mol | Cl = 71.65/35.45 = 2.021 mol

Step 3: Divide by smallest value (2.021)
H : C : Cl = 2 : 1 : 1

Step 4: Empirical Formula = CH₂Cl

Step 5: Empirical mass = 12.01 + 2(1.008) + 35.45 = 49.48 g

Step 6: n = 98.96 / 49.48 = 2

Molecular Formula = CH₂Cl × 2 = C₂H₄Cl₂
