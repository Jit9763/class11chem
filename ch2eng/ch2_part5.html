2.10 Rutherford's Atomic Model (रदरफोर्ड का परमाणु मॉडल)

Based on the brilliant conclusions of his alpha-particle scattering experiment, Ernest Rutherford proposed a nuclear model of the atom in 1911. It is often referred to as the Planetary Model (ग्रहीय मॉडल). The main postulates are:

  • The Nucleus (नाभिक): The entire positive charge and almost all the mass of the atom are densely concentrated in an incredibly small region at the center. Rutherford called it the "Nucleus".
  • Electron Orbits (इलेक्ट्रॉन की कक्षाएँ): The negatively charged electrons revolve around the nucleus with very high speeds in circular paths called orbits. This is exactly how planets revolve around the sun.
  • Electrical Attraction (विद्युत आकर्षण): Electrons and the nucleus are held together by strong electrostatic forces of attraction, acting just like gravitational pull between planets.
रदरफोर्ड के अनुसार: सारा प्लस चार्ज और द्रव्यमान बीच में एक छोटे से 'नाभिक' में है, और इलेक्ट्रॉन इसके चारों ओर तेज़ी से गोल कक्षा में ঘুরते हैं (जैसे सूर्य के चारों ओर ग्रह)।

Size Comparison (आकार की तुलना): A Mind-Blowing Fact!

The radius of the atom is about 10-10 m, while the radius of the nucleus is only about 10-15 m!
This means the nucleus is 100,000 times (1 लाख गुना) smaller than the atom!

Analogy: If you imagine the nucleus as a cricket ball (क्रिकेट की गेंद), the entire atom would be the size of a massive 5-kilometer radius stadium! And 99.9% of that 5 km stadium would just be empty space!

2.11 Drawbacks of Rutherford's Model (रदरफोर्ड मॉडल की कमियां)

Rutherford’s model was the most sophisticated at the time, but soon, fundamental physical laws placed a massive question mark (?) on its validity!

What went wrong? (Maxwell's Theory):

According to the Electromagnetic theory proposed by the great physicist James Clark Maxwell:

"Whenever a charged particle is subjected to acceleration, it continuously emits electromagnetic radiation (loses energy)."
  • We know an electron is a charged particle (-).
  • And science states that whenever something moves in a circle, its direction continuously changes, meaning it is undergoing acceleration (त्वरण)!
मैक्सवेल के सिद्धांत के अनुसार: जब भी कोई आवेशित कण (इलेक्ट्रॉन) गोल घूमता है (त्वरण में होता है), तो वह लगातार अपनी ऊर्जा (Energy) बाहर निकालता है।

The Spiral Collapse (परमाणु का पतन):

If Rutherford is strictly correct, the revolving electron will continuously radiate energy. As its energy decreases, its speed will drop, and the positively charged nucleus will drag it closer.

The electron's orbit will shrink, becoming a spiral path (सर्पिलाकार). Calculations showed that in just 10-8 seconds, the electron would crash straight into the nucleus! 💥 BOOM!! The atom would collapse entirely.

Conclusion: Rutherford's model completely failed to explain the Stability of the Atom (परमाणु का स्थायित्व)! (Because atoms obviously do not collapse).
