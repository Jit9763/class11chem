2.5 Discovery of Protons (प्रोटॉन की खोज)

Even after the discovery of the electron, scientists knew that atoms are overall electrically neutral (विद्युत उदासीन). This meant that if an atom contains negative (-) charge, there must logically be an equal amount of positive (+) charge hidden inside to balance it!

चूँकि परमाणु विद्युत उदासीन होता है, इसलिए वैज्ञानिकों को यकीन था कि इसमें माइनस को बैलेंस करने के लिए कोई प्लस चार्ज भी ज़रूर छिपा होगा।

Goldstein's Experiment (1886):

  • E. Goldstein slightly modified Thomson's Cathode Ray Tube. He used a special cathode that had holes (छेद) in it, known as a perforated cathode (छिद्रित कैथोड).
  • When high voltage was passed, he observed that some rays were traveling in the opposite direction (उल्टी दिशा)! They originated from the anode, passed through the holes of the cathode, and produced a glow on the back wall.
  • Since these rays passed through the canals (holes), Goldstein named them Canal Rays (कैनाल किरणें) or Anode Rays.
गोल्डस्टीन ने छिद्र वाले कैथोड का उपयोग किया और देखा कि कुछ किरणें एनोड से कैथोड के छेदों में से पार होकर उल्टी दिशा में जा रही थीं। इन किरणों को कैनाल किरणें कहा गया।

Experiments revealed that the particles making up these rays are positively charged (धनावेशित). When hydrogen gas was used, the lightest and smallest positive particle was obtained. This was later named the Proton (प्रोटॉन).

Charge on Proton (प्रोटॉन पर आवेश)
+1.6022 × 10-19 C
(Exactly equal to an electron, but just positive)
Mass of Proton (प्रोटॉन का द्रव्यमान)
1.672 × 10-27 kg

2.6 Discovery of Neutrons (न्यूट्रॉन की खोज)

After finding protons and electrons, a new problem arose! When scientists measured the total mass of an atom, it was significantly higher than the combined mass of its protons and electrons. This clearly indicated that there is another 'hidden' heavy particle inside the atom that has no electrical charge (कोई विद्युत आवेश नहीं) (otherwise it wouldn't be neutral).

जब परमाणु का कुल द्रव्यमान नापा गया, तो वह केवल प्रोटॉन और इलेक्ट्रॉन के योग से ज़्यादा था। इसका मतलब अंदर बिना चार्ज वाला कोई भारी कण भी मौजूद था!

Chadwick's Experiment (1932):

  • James Chadwick performed an experiment where he bombarded (बमबारी की) a thin sheet of Beryllium (बेरिलियम) metal with high-speed Alpha (α) particles.
  • Upon collision, highly penetrating (अत्यधिक भेदने वाली) rays were emitted from the beryllium.
  • The most important observation was that these new rays were unaffected (प्रभावित नहीं) by magnetic or electric fields! This meant they carried zero charge.
चैडविक ने बेरिलियम पर अल्फा कणों की बमबारी की, जिससे बिना चार्ज वाली अत्यधिक भेदने वाली किरणें निकलीं।

Chadwick proved that these rays consisted of neutral (उदासीन) particles. These neutral particles were named Neutrons (न्यूट्रॉन), and their mass was found to be almost equal to that of a proton.

Charge on Neutron (न्यूट्रॉन पर आवेश)
0 (Neutral)
Mass of Neutron (न्यूट्रॉन का द्रव्यमान)
1.674 × 10-27 kg
(Slightly heavier than a proton)
